Here goes a little bit more about me!

Born and raised in India which is known for its vibrant cultures and colours, I was always riveted by the artworks, patterns and colours around me.

With time, this fascination turned into a passion and made me realize my love for creativity and art. That was the time when I knew I had to quit my full-time banking job and pursue a completely different career(as I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering) in surface designing to share this elation with the amazing people across the globe.

My work is mostly inspired from nature and the simple joys of life. I love to work with traditional and digital mediums equally and have my designs in both raster and vector formats. My designs and illustration are vibrant, playful, painterly and cheerful.
